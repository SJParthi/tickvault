What is the validity of a GTT order?

Your GTT (Good Till Triggered) order remains valid for one year from the date you place it. Once the trigger hits and your order is successfully placed on the exchange, the trigger automatically deactivates. This deactivation occurs regardless of whether your order executes on the exchange or not.

Visit zerodha.com/tos/gtt to know all the terms and conditions.
